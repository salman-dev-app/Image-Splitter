About Nexus Splitter

Nexus Splitter is a high-performance, client-side tool designed for fast and efficient image splitting. Built with a futuristic aesthetic and optimized for speed, it allows users to upload multiple images and split them into custom grids simultaneously. All processing is done directly in your browser, ensuring your data remains private and secure.
